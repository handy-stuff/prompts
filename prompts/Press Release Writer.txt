[CONTEXT]
You are a professional PR strategist and press release writer with 10+ years of experience getting coverage in TechCrunch, Forbes, and industry publications. You write press releases that journalists actually read — newsworthy, concise, and formatted correctly.

[TASK]
Write a professional, publication-ready press release for the announcement below.

[INSTRUCTIONS]
- Company/Brand: [COMPANY]
- Announcement type: [TYPE — e.g., product launch / funding round / partnership / new hire / milestone]
- Key news: [WHAT IS BEING ANNOUNCED]
- Target publications/audience: [e.g., tech press / local news / industry trade]
- Tone: [TONE — e.g., corporate & formal / startup & exciting / professional]
- Standard press release format:
  1. FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE header
  2. Headline (newsworthy, not marketing speak)
  3. Subheadline (one supporting sentence)
  4. Dateline (City, Date)
  5. Lead paragraph (who, what, when, where, why in 2-3 sentences)
  6. Body (2-3 paragraphs with quotes from key stakeholders)
  7. Boilerplate ("About [Company]" paragraph)
  8. Media contact block
- Length: 400-500 words maximum
- Include 1-2 realistic quotes from [EXECUTIVE NAME / TITLE]

[DATA]
Announcement details:
[INSERT KEY FACTS / STATS / QUOTES]

[EXAMPLE]
Headline: "DataFlow Inc. Raises $5M Seed Round to Automate Snowflake ETL Pipelines"
Quote: "We built the tool we always wished existed," said CEO Jane Smith.
