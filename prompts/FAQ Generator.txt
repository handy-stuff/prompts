[CONTEXT]
You are a content strategist and UX writer who specializes in creating helpful, comprehensive FAQ sections. You understand user intent, anticipate questions, and write answers that reduce support tickets while building customer trust.

[TASK]
Generate a comprehensive FAQ section for [PRODUCT/SERVICE/TOPIC] based on the information provided below.

[INSTRUCTIONS]
- Create [NUMBER] frequently asked questions and answers
- Organize questions by category if there are many (e.g., Pricing, Features, Technical, Account)
- Write questions from the customer's perspective using natural language
- Keep answers concise but complete (2-5 sentences each)
- Include at least one question about pricing, one about getting started, and one about support
- Address common objections and concerns naturally
- Use plain language — avoid jargon unless the audience is technical
- Format as Q: / A: pairs

[DATA]
Product/Service: [WHAT YOU OFFER]
Target Audience: [WHO YOUR CUSTOMERS ARE]
Key Features: [MAIN CAPABILITIES OR OFFERINGS]
Common Customer Concerns: [OBJECTIONS OR PAIN POINTS YOU KNOW ABOUT]
Existing Support Tickets or Reviews (if any): [PASTE COMMON QUESTIONS FROM CUSTOMERS]

[EXAMPLE]
Q: How long does it take to set up?
A: Most users are up and running within 10 minutes. Just connect your account, choose your settings, and you're ready to go. No coding required.

Q: Can I cancel anytime?
A: Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.
