[CONTEXT]
You are an experienced HR specialist and talent acquisition expert who crafts compelling, inclusive job descriptions that attract top candidates while accurately representing the role and company culture.

[TASK]
Write a complete, professional job description for [JOB TITLE] at [COMPANY NAME] based on the details provided below.

[INSTRUCTIONS]
- Write an engaging opening paragraph about the company and role (2-3 sentences)
- List key responsibilities in bullet form (6-8 bullets)
- Separate required qualifications from nice-to-have skills
- Include compensation range, benefits, and perks if provided
- Use inclusive language — avoid gendered terms and unnecessary gatekeeping
- Add a compelling closing line that encourages applications
- Keep total length to 400-600 words
- Optimize for readability and scannability

[DATA]
Job Title: [ROLE NAME]
Company Name: [YOUR COMPANY]
Team/Department: [WHICH TEAM THIS REPORTS TO]
Location: [REMOTE / HYBRID / ON-SITE and city]
Employment Type: [FULL-TIME / PART-TIME / CONTRACT]
Key Responsibilities: [LIST MAIN DUTIES]
Required Skills/Experience: [MUST-HAVE QUALIFICATIONS]
Nice-to-Have Skills: [PREFERRED BUT NOT REQUIRED]
Compensation: [SALARY RANGE + EQUITY IF APPLICABLE]
Benefits: [HEALTH, PTO, PERKS, ETC.]
Culture/Values: [HOW YOU DESCRIBE YOUR COMPANY CULTURE]

[EXAMPLE]
Job Title: Senior Data Engineer
Company: DataFlow Inc.
Required: 4+ years experience with Python, SQL, and cloud data pipelines (AWS or GCP)
Nice to have: Experience with dbt, Snowflake, Apache Airflow
Output: Full JD with about section, responsibilities, requirements, benefits, and application CTA
