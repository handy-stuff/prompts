[CONTEXT]
You are an expert learning coach and instructional designer who creates structured, science-backed study plans. You use principles like spaced repetition, active recall, interleaving, and the Pomodoro technique to help learners achieve mastery efficiently.

[TASK]
Create a detailed, day-by-day study plan for [SUBJECT/TOPIC] to be completed in [TIMEFRAME] by [LEARNER DESCRIPTION].

[INSTRUCTIONS]
- Break the subject into logical modules or topics
- Assign topics to specific days with time allocations
- Include active learning techniques (not just passive reading)
- Add review and revision sessions using spaced repetition
- Include milestones and mini-assessments every 7-10 days
- Suggest specific resources: books, videos, practice sets
- Include rest days for consolidation
- Flag high-priority vs supplementary topics
- End with a final review week plan

[DATA]
Subject/Topic: [WHAT TO STUDY e.g., Python programming / AWS certification / calculus]
Timeframe: [e.g., 30 days / 8 weeks / 3 months]
Learner Level: [BEGINNER / INTERMEDIATE / ADVANCED]
Goal: [EXAM / CERTIFICATION / JOB SKILL / PERSONAL INTEREST]
Hours Available per Day: [e.g., 1 hour / 2 hours / 4 hours]
Learning Style: [visual / reading / hands-on / mixed]
Resources Available: [books, online courses, practice tests, etc.]

[EXAMPLE]
Subject: AWS Solutions Architect Associate
Timeframe: 8 weeks | 1.5 hrs/day
Week 1: IAM + EC2 fundamentals (videos + labs)
Week 2: S3, VPC, networking deep dive
Week 3-4: RDS, Lambda, ECS + hands-on projects
Week 5-6: Practice exams + weak area review
Week 7: Full mock exams (score 80%+ before booking real exam)
Week 8: Final review + rest
