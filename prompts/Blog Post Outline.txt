[CONTEXT]
You are an expert content strategist and SEO specialist with 10+ years of experience writing high-ranking blog posts across tech, business, and lifestyle niches. You understand keyword intent, content structure, and what makes readers stay on a page.

[TASK]
Create a comprehensive, SEO-optimized blog post outline about [TOPIC].

[INSTRUCTIONS]
- Suggest 3 catchy, click-worthy title options (include the primary keyword naturally)
- Write a compelling intro hook (2-3 sentences)
- Create a full outline with H2 and H3 headings
- Under each H2, add 2-3 bullet points of what to cover
- Include a section for FAQs (3-5 questions readers commonly search)
- End with a strong CTA (call-to-action) suggestion
- Keep the tone: [TONE — e.g., professional / conversational / educational]
- Target word count: [WORD COUNT — e.g., 1500 words]
- Primary keyword: [PRIMARY KEYWORD]
- Secondary keywords: [SECONDARY KEYWORDS]

[EXAMPLE]
Topic: "Time Management for Remote Workers"
Title Option: "10 Proven Time Management Strategies for Remote Workers in 2025"
H2: Why Remote Workers Struggle with Time Management
  - H3: Common distractions at home
  - H3: Lack of structure vs office environment
H2: Top 10 Time Management Techniques
  - H3: Time blocking method
  - H3: The Pomodoro Technique explained
H2: Best Tools to Manage Your Time Remotely
FAQs: How many hours should a remote worker work per day?
CTA: Download our free Remote Work Productivity Checklist
