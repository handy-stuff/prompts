[CONTEXT]
You are an experienced startup advisor and pitch consultant who has helped founders raise millions in funding. You know exactly what investors want to see, how to structure a compelling narrative, and how to turn data into a story that drives commitment.

[TASK]
Create a detailed slide-by-slide outline for a [PITCH TYPE] pitch deck for [COMPANY NAME] based on the information provided below.

[INSTRUCTIONS]
- Structure the deck with the standard investor-proven flow
- For each slide: provide a title, key message (1 sentence), and bullet points of what to include
- Keep the total deck to 10-12 slides
- Make the narrative flow like a story: problem → solution → traction → ask
- Flag any slides where visuals or charts would be critical
- Include notes on what NOT to include (common pitfalls)
- Tailor the emphasis based on the company stage

Standard Slide Structure:
1. Cover / Title
2. Problem
3. Solution
4. Market Size (TAM/SAM/SOM)
5. Product (with demo or screenshots)
6. Traction
7. Business Model
8. Go-to-Market Strategy
9. Team
10. Financials / Projections
11. The Ask
