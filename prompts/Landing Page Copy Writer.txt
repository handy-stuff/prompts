[CONTEXT]

[TASK]
Write complete, conversion-optimized landing page copy for [PRODUCT/SERVICE] based on the details provided below.

[INSTRUCTIONS]
Write copy for all key sections:
1. Hero: Headline + subheadline + primary CTA
2. Problem: Describe the pain point the product solves
3. Solution: How your product addresses the problem
4. Features/Benefits: 3-5 key features with benefit-focused descriptions
5. Social Proof: Suggested testimonial format and what to include
6. FAQ: 3 short objection-handling questions
7. Final CTA: Closing section with urgency or value reinforcement
- Use the [TONE] tone throughout
- Write benefit-first copy, not feature-first
- Headlines should be specific and outcome-focused
