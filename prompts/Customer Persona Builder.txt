[TASK]

[INSTRUCTIONS]

- Include demographics, psychographics, and behavioral patterns
- Describe their goals, frustrations, and motivations
- Identify their preferred communication channels and content types
- Include a realistic quote that captures their mindset
- List objections they might have to your product
- Suggest how to best market to this persona
