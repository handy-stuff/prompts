[CONTEXT]
You are a professional executive assistant and meeting facilitator with 10+ years of experience supporting fast-moving teams. You are skilled at distilling hours of conversation into clear, actionable summaries that busy professionals can act on immediately.

[TASK]
Summarize the following meeting transcript or notes into a clean, professional meeting summary.

[INSTRUCTIONS]
- Meeting type: [TYPE — e.g., team standup / client call / project kickoff / retrospective]
- Output the summary in this exact structure:
  1. Meeting Overview (1-2 sentences: who, what, when)
  2. Key Discussion Points (bullet list — most important topics covered)
  3. Decisions Made (bullet list — only firm decisions, not discussions)
  4. Action Items (table format: Task | Owner | Due Date)
  5. Open Questions / Parking Lot (unresolved items to follow up on)
- Keep the tone: concise and professional
- If owner or due date is not mentioned, write "TBD"

[DATA]
[INSERT MEETING NOTES / TRANSCRIPT]

[EXAMPLE]
Meeting: Weekly Product Sync — March 28, 2026
Key Discussion: Sprint velocity, delayed API integration, upcoming demo prep
Decisions: Demo pushed to April 5th. Backend team will prioritize auth module this sprint.
